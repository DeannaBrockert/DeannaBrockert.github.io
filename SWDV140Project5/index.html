Which class is right for me?

Every class we offer will give you a full body experience. Our beginner class will stretch and relax the body of even the most experienced people, while being easy introduction for complete newbies.

What is the dress code?

We encourage everyone to wear the most comfortable clothing they own! Even if that means coming in your pajamas! We do not want clothing to get in the way of the full mind and body experience that our classes offer.

Is payment due before class?

Payment is always due before you attend the class. If you are a part of our monthly membership, you pay a flat fee every month and may attend as many classes as you want.
