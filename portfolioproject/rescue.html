Rescue Work

Over the past 3 years, I have been volunteering at a local animal rescue.
In that time, I have rescued and helped home over 130 cats.
That may sound crazy to some, but it has been such a joy and so fulfilling to help these animals.
I have seen cats and kittens come into our rescue that have been beaten, burned, shot, poisened, hit by cars, or were incredibly ill.
To see them go from immense suffering to living happy and healthy lives in their forever homes is the best feeling.
